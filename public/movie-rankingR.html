My Top 3 Movie/Series Recommendations – Ruby

They're 3 movies

The Straw

This movie touched me deeply. It’s one of the most emotional films I’ve ever watched. I cried throughout the entire story. It brings out raw emotions, vulnerability, and reminds us how fragile life and human relationships can be. I recommend it to anyone who needs a reminder of how powerful empathy can be.

The Sinners

This film really got me thinking. It has a dark, mysterious vibe, and a part of it feels strangely real. Honestly, I believe vampires might truly exist and walk among us. The story explores themes of secrets, hidden identities, and the line between good and evil. It’s chilling, but so fascinating.

The Hate U Give

This is a powerful and emotional film about racial injustice. It tells the story of a Black teenager who finds her voice after witnessing police violence. The film reflects the reality that many Black communities face — and it does it with raw truth and emotional power.
